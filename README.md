## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. Smart Search Engine
Effortlessly search for scholarships across various countries, universities, funding level, related information,... Our system aggregates data to provide you with the most relevant opportunities based on your keywords.

![Search Feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DieppDiepp/ScholarshipRouting/main/image_for_readme/search_feature.png)

### 2. Advanced Filtering System
Don't get overwhelmed by too much information. Our filter feature allows you to narrow down scholarships by:
* **Country** (e.g., UK, USA, Australia)
* **Level of Study** (Bachelor, Master, PhD)
* **Field of Study** (Major)
* **Scholarship Type** (Government, University, Organization)
* **Funding Type** (Full, Partial,...)

![Filter Feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DieppDiepp/ScholarshipRouting/main/image_for_readme/filter_feature.png)

### 3. Detailed Scholarship Insights
View comprehensive details for every scholarship, including funding value, deadlines, eligibility criteria, and direct links to official application portals.

![View Detail Feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DieppDiepp/ScholarshipRouting/main/image_for_readme/viewdetail_feature.png)

### 4. AI-Powered Chatbot Consultant
Integrate with Gemini and RAG technology, our AI Chatbot acts as your personal consultant. It can:
* Answer specific questions about scholarship requirements.
* Compare different scholarship programs.
* Provide personalized advice based on your profile.

![Chatbot Feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DieppDiepp/ScholarshipRouting/main/image_for_readme/chat-bot_feature.png)
![Chatbot Feature 2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DieppDiepp/ScholarshipRouting/main/image_for_readme/chat-bot-2_feature.png)

### 5. Application Tracking Dashboard
Keep your scholarship hunting organized. Track the status of your applications (Saved, Submitted, Under Review, Accepted) all in one place.

![Application Track Feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DieppDiepp/ScholarshipRouting/main/image_for_readme/application-track_feature.png)
